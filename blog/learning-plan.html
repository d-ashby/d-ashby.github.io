learning plan

What is your long term goal or career pathway?

To become skilled enough to land a job as a software developer, it's too early to say specialties i want to focus on. Fundamentally I want to be in a position where I am able to learn more and be with other people who love to learn too.

A description of your strengths and limitations concerning learning

My strength is my drive to learn, my inquisitiveness, I suppose that is also my limitation as I realise I will need to keep focused on what really matters at the time. I also need to work on the core side of my skills not just technical!

What skills (non-technical - human skills) would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

I would like to build on my interpersonal skills and confidence with being out of my comfort zone socially. Be more social!

how you will manage yourself to work productively and safely with other learners, facilitators and industry/community representatives?

I find it easier to learn when my body is kept healthy. Taking regular breaks, exercise, good sleep, and a healthy diet are my aims for my physical wellbeing (something I struggled with!). I hope that I will be able to keep my mental wellbeing healthy through being social and connecting with others, and be present for others who seek my council. If I keep these things in check I will be the best me for peers, facilitators and all others I will be interacting with during this bootcamp.

What you will do when things are building up and starting to get to you?

I hope that by making a commitment and keeping to the steps I mentioned above, I won’t reach this point. But if I feel like I am losing my footing, I will have to reassess my position and seek advice from my peers and facilitators.

A commitment as to how and where you will seek help in a timely way

I will do my best to communicate any failings on my side. I will do what I can to contribute in the tenacity and success of the team.

A description of what you expect from the facilitation team

I expect the facilitators to guide and facilitate learning.

Any scheduling information such as block-out times when you are committed to other things. For example, you might have whānau commitments like school drop-offs. It's important to plan these in.

I am lucky enough to give my full attention during this bootcamp.
